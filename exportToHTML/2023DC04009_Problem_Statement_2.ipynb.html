By accurately predicting which customers are likely to churn, companies can take proactive measuressuch as targeted marketing campaigns, personalized offers, or improved customer serviceto enhance customer satisfaction and retention.

886   
887  - **Accuracy:** The overall correctness of the model. 
888  - **Precision:** The proportion of predicted churners that are actual churners. 
889  - **Recall:** The proportion of actual churners that the model successfully identifies. 
890  - **F1-Score:** The harmonic mean of precision and recall, offering a balance between them. 
891  - **AUC-ROC:** The model's ability to distinguish between churn and non-churn cases.

915     - In churn prediction, the cost of not identifying a churner (false negative) is typically far more damaging than the cost of falsely predicting churn (false positive).
